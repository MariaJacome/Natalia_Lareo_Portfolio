Lopapeysa by Andrés Velencoso x Martin Miller's Gin

Creative & Art Direction

This project consists of the creation of a special exclusive pack together with Andrés Velencoso, this pack includes a handmade wool sweater Lopapeysa style, which seeks to reflect the Icelandic origin of the water with which Martin Miller's gin is created.
