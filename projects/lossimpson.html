Los Simpson

Creative & Art Direction

In one of the episodes of the latest season of The Simpsons, Homer decides to make a paella. With ingredients such as chorizo among those used, the users of our networks soon realised that the recipe was a real nonsense. However, there is one ingredient that Homer hits the nail on the head. For his paella he uses Calasparr rice.

So we contacted an expert in micro-art to give him the tribute he deserves, Hasan Kale was commissioned to paint a portrait of the character on one of the grains of rice from Calasparra, which was later transferred to the town hall where it is on display.
