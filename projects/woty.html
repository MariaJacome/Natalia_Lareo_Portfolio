Glamour Women of the Year

Creative & Art Direction

Art and creative direction for the first edition of WOTY Spain. We sought to evoke the movement created by women in the world through an element that characterizes them as much as flowers. This identity is totally liquid and this movement in the graphics links us directly to the location, one of the most famous music venues in Spain, La Riviera. In this way, we developed a visual identity with a very marked beauty and a modern aesthetic. Creating a series of communication pieces that maintain this common thread throughout the entire physical and digital experience.
